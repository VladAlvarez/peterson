Accepted Insurance and Payment Options:

We currently accept PreferredOne, Medicare, and self-pay. If you plan to use insurance, we recommend contacting your provider before your initial visit to confirm your benefits, deductibles, co-insurance, and co-pays for mental health coverage.

If your insurance is not listed, please inquire with your provider about "out-of-network benefits." We can provide a superbill and submit the claim to your insurance company.

For self-pay clients, our fees are:

  • $175 for an initial session
  • $100 for a 45 to 50-minute session
  • $145 for sessions over 55 minutes

Jessica Peterson

LICSW

Jessica Peterson provides individual, couples, and family counseling for adults, with a specialty in treating caregivers and healthcare professionals. She also facilitates parenting groups, domestic violence groups, and workshops.

As an adjunct professor, Jessica teaches MSW students, sharing her extensive knowledge and experience in the field.

Lance Peterson

PhD, LICSW

Lance Peterson offers individual, couples, and family counseling for adults and teens, specializing in treating healthcare professionals. As a full-time professor at the University of St. Thomas, he teaches clinical skills to professionals.

Lance is proficient in solution-focused therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy, and emotionally focused couples therapy. For more information, visit his University of St. Thomas profile.
